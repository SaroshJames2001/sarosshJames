Thalassemia App

The "Thalassemia Care Network" project aims to develop a web-based platform and a mobile application to connect thalassemia patients in need with potential donors. The platform will facilitate the registration and management of donors, enable doctors to enter and access patient data, and allow donors to locate nearby thalassemia patients and schedule donations. The project's outcome will be a functional and secure system that enhances support for thalassemia patients and encourages community engagement in their care.
